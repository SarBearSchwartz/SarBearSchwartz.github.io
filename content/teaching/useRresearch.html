Have you heard about R, but don’t know where to start? After trying out R, are you still overwhelmed? Do you want to take your quantitative analysis to the next level? If so, then this is the class for you!

8 Wednesdays in Fall 2024 September (4, 11, 18, 25) & October (2, 9, 23, 30) 1:30 – 2:20 pm DistEd 109 or Zoom

Open all faculty and grad students in CEHS Come learn hands-on in a lab setting during 8-weeks of step-by-step instruction on: * managing and reshaping data * quickly creating descriptive tables * exploratory and advanced plotting * transparent quantitative modeling * creating supplemental materials

You may use R to work with your own data and become empowered to learn more advanced topics about R using available resources via various websites and books. Some statistical topics will be discussed in reference to R code but a full understanding of the methods is not required.
